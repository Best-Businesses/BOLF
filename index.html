Organization strategy

Our organization plans to lease and acquire land, which will be certified for agricultural production. The land will provide housing for homeless individuals who will participate in farm operations. The farm will supply fresh produce to food pantries and sell agriculture products & goods at fair market value. We will also produce and sell compost and fertilizer. Participants will be encouraged to enroll in online college courses to further their education. Upon successful completion of the program, individuals will receive permanent land to call home, along with assistance in constructing a tiny home.

Our life-transforming model

Permanent Housing

Not a shelter. private, safe, housing on farmland for every resident.

Meaningful Employment

Paid farm work — produce for food banks, markets, and agriculture product sales.

Online College

Land Forever + Help building a home

Mentors & “Open Ear” Companions

We need mentors for homeless people and essentially just need people to be an open ear. Many future residents have experienced isolation and trauma. We need Christian mentors willing to walk alongside them — to listen, pray, share life skills, and offer consistent emotional support.

  • ✨ One-on-one discipleship & encouragement
  • ✨ Assist with goal setting (education, finances, health)
  • ✨ Just being an open ear without judgment

Farm & Facility Builders

We also need volunteers to help build campsites, trails, for kitchen duties, etc. Before residents arrive, we need to construct the physical heart of the community.

  • 🏕️ Building primitive campsites for retreats & transitional phases
  • 🌿 Creating accessible nature trails across farmland
  • 🍲 Kitchen duties: preparing meals for volunteer crews and future community dinners
  • 🚜 Erosion control, fencing, greenhouse assembly
  • ♻️ Compost station construction & gardening infrastructure

No experience necessary — just a willing heart and hands!
